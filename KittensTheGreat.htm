Story


You are a Kitty, you want to be left alone. You HATE people. They've done some very bad things.
One of the worst things they've ever done, was vote against you One thousand, one hundred, and one times, in the most recent Ludum Dare theme voting.
Get them back now, travel to three different cities, and decimate the population. Then you can finally be alone.

Controls


Arrow Keys to move.
All actions are explained on-screen. Basically keep moving and pressing X.
Cmon its a board game. How hard could it be?

Background


This game was made within 48 hours for Ludum Dare 21. This was my fourth 48-hour game, and although I feel I wasted a lot of time, and could have made this into something much better, I feel satisfied I was able to finish in time.
